Featured Projects

Arrest Diversion

Evaluating pre-arrest diversion programs that connect individuals with substance use disorder to treatment rather than incarceration. Studies of MARI and CARES demonstrate significant reductions in recidivism, overdose deaths, and incarceration.

Substance Use Disorder Program Evaluation Simulation
View publications & coverage →

Predicting Recidivism

Developing and evaluating quantitative methods for forecasting recidivism and assessing crime prediction tools, including the NIJ Recidivism Forecasting Challenge and improvements to hot-spot mapping metrics for evidence-based criminal justice decisions.

Machine Learning Crime Forecasting NIJ
View publications & coverage →

Crisis Response Models

Mathematical modeling of alternative police response frameworks for mental health crisis calls, evaluating co-responder and mobile crisis unit deployments to optimize resource allocation and improve outcomes for individuals in crisis.

Simulation Mental Health Policing
