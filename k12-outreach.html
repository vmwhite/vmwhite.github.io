K–12 OR/MS Outreach

Increasing underrepresented students' exposure to operations research and data science

Overview

Operations research and management science (OR/MS) remain underrepresented fields among students from historically marginalized communities, limiting both diversity within the discipline and the range of problems it tackles. This initiative develops and deploys adaptable K–12 outreach curricula that introduce students to OR/MS concepts in inclusive, accessible ways. Funded through INFORMS, the project partners with local schools to run hands-on activities that expose future decision-makers to the field early and position it as a welcoming career path for all students.
